Global sourcing, QA verification, and supply of premium singlemode & multimode optical fibre

About Our Fibre Division

BH TECH CONSULTING INC. has expanded into global optical fibre sourcing and distribution, partnering directly with high-capacity manufacturers in China and India. We supply raw optical fibre reels to telecom operators, cable manufacturers, infrastructure projects, and private organizations across the UAE, Africa, and Asia.

Available Fibre Types

ITU-T G.652.D

Standard singlemode fibre for long-distance and metropolitan networks.

ITU-T G.657.A1

Bend-insensitive fibre for drop networks, FTTX and high-density deployments.

ITU-T G.657.A2

Ultra bend-insensitive fibre with superior macro-bend performance.

OM2 Multimode

850nm optimized multimode fibre for short-reach LAN backbone applications.

OM3 Multimode

Laser-optimized 850nm multimode fibre for 10G/40G/100G networks.

OM4 Multimode

High-performance multimode fibre for extended 100G reach and premium data centers.

Quality Assurance

All fibre supplied undergoes full geometry inspection, attenuation testing at 1310/1550 nm, batch verification, and optional OTDR trace reporting. Quality reports can be shared with clients prior to shipment.

Global Logistics

We ship directly from manufacturing plants in China and India to ports in UAE, Africa, and Asia. Custom reel lengths (50km, 100km, 250km) are available. Annual supply capacity: 15 million km singlemode + 10 million km multimode.
