Optical Fibre

Optical Fibre Supply – Global Capacity & Models

BH Tech provides bulk optical fibre directly from partner factories in China and India, with a commercial structure tailored for carriers, ISPs, cable manufacturers and integrators.

Available Fibre Types

Singlemode

  • ITU-T G.652D – standard singlemode
  • ITU-T G.657A1 – bend-insensitive, compatible with G.652D
  • ITU-T G.657A2 – ultra bend-insensitive for high-density and last-mile

Multimode

  • OM2 – 50/125 µm multimode fibre
  • OM3 – laser-optimised multimode for 10G+
  • OM4 – extended-reach multimode for 40G/100G links

Capacity & Logistics

  • Annual capacity via BH Tech:
    up to 15 million km singlemode & 10 million km multimode
  • Custom reel lengths: 25 km, 50 km, 100 km, 250 km+ as per design
  • Labelling & packing: factory or customer brand, export-ready packaging
  • Delivery regions: UAE, GCC, Iraq, Africa, Central Asia (others on request)

Quality Assurance & Documentation

  • Factory certificates and technical parameter sheets for each model
  • OTDR traces and test reports provided for production batches on request
  • Batch numbers and drum labels aligned with customer tracking systems
  • Option to align with client-specific acceptance criteria for attenuation, geometry and PMD

To discuss volume pricing, annual frameworks or project-based supply, contact us to request a fibre quotation or OTDR samples.
